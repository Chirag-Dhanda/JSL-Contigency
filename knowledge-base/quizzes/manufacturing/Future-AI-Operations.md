# Question 1

### What is the primary objective of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in manufacturing?

- A. Increase manual work
- B. Improve efficiency, quality, and decision-making
- C. Replace all employees immediately
- D. Reduce product quality

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

AI analyzes production data, automates repetitive tasks, and supports better operational decisions.

---

# Question 2

### What is Industry 4.0?

- A. A type of steel
- B. The fourth industrial revolution based on connected and intelligent manufacturing
- C. A warehouse management system
- D. A quality inspection method

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Industry 4.0 combines AI, IoT, robotics, cloud computing, and automation to create smart factories.

# Question 5

### What is Predictive Maintenance?

- A. Repairing machines after they fail
- B. Replacing every machine each year
- C. Using data to predict equipment failures before they occur
- D. Cleaning machines daily

**Correct Answer:** C

**Explanation:**

Predictive Maintenance uses sensor data and AI to identify potential failures, reducing downtime and maintenance costs.

# Question 6

### What is a Digital Twin?

- A. A duplicate machine used for backup
- B. A virtual model of a physical machine or process
- C. A warehouse storage system
- D. A barcode label

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

A Digital Twin simulates real equipment or processes, allowing engineers to test changes and optimize performance.

# Question 8

### A sensor reports abnormal vibration in a rolling mill, and AI predicts a bearing failure within the next 48 hours. What should happen next?

- A. Ignore the alert
- B. Schedule maintenance before the failure occurs
- C. Continue production until the machine stops
- D. Replace the entire production line

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Predictive Maintenance allows planned repairs before equipment fails, reducing downtime and preventing costly breakdowns.

- B. IoT
- C. Robotics and Cloud Computing
- D. All of the Above

**Explanation:**

Smart factories combine multiple digital technologies to automate operations, improve decision-making, and optimize production.

# Question 10

### Which statement best describes the future of AI in manufacturing?

- A. AI will eliminate every manufacturing job.
- B. AI will support employees by improving efficiency, safety, and decision-making.
- C. AI is only useful for office work.
- D. AI has no role in steel manufacturing.

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

AI is intended to complement human expertise by automating repetitive tasks, improving safety, and providing data-driven insights rather than replacing every worker.
