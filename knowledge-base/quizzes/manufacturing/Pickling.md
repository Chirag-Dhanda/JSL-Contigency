# Question 1

### What is the primary purpose of the Pickling process?

- A. To melt stainless steel
- B. To remove oxide scale and surface impurities
- C. To package finished products
- D. To reduce steel thickness

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Pickling removes oxide scale, rust, and other surface contaminants formed during hot rolling and heat treatment.

---

# Question 2

### Which chemicals are commonly used in the Pickling process?

- A. Water and Oil
- B. Hydrochloric Acid and Nitric Acid
- C. Nitric Acid and Hydrofluoric Acid
- D. Sulfuric Acid and Diesel

**Correct Answer:** C

**Explanation:**

Nitric Acid and Hydrofluoric Acid are commonly used together to remove oxide layers from stainless steel surfaces.

---

# Question 3

### Which manufacturing process takes place immediately before Pickling?

- A. Dispatch
- B. Hot Rolling
- C. Grinding
- D. Warehouse

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

After Hot Rolling, oxide scale forms on the steel surface, which is then removed during Pickling.

---

# Question 4

### What is removed during the Pickling process?

- A. Carbon
- B. Oxide Scale
- C. Alloying Elements
- D. Packaging Material

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

The acid solution dissolves the oxide scale without damaging the stainless steel.

---

# Question 5

### Why is Pickling important?

- A. It improves surface cleanliness.
- B. It prepares steel for further processing.
- C. It improves corrosion resistance.
- D. All of the above.

**Correct Answer:** D

**Explanation:**

Pickling cleans the steel surface, improves corrosion resistance, and prepares it for processes such as Cold Rolling.

---

# Question 6

### Which equipment is commonly used in a Pickling Line?

- A. Rolling Mill
- B. Acid Tanks
- C. Cooling Tower
- D. Electric Arc Furnace

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Acid tanks contain the pickling solution that removes scale and surface impurities.

---

# Question 7

### Which process usually follows Pickling?

- A. Cold Rolling
- B. Sales
- C. Dispatch
- D. Scrap Management

**Correct Answer:** A

**Explanation:**

After Pickling, the cleaned steel is sent for Cold Rolling to achieve the required thickness and finish.

---

# Question 8

### Why is rinsing performed after Pickling?

- A. To remove remaining acid from the steel surface
- B. To increase steel temperature
- C. To melt the steel again
- D. To polish the steel

**Correct Answer:** A

**Explanation:**

Rinsing removes residual acid, preventing corrosion and ensuring the steel is safe for further processing.

---

# Question 9

### Which Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) is especially important during Pickling operations?

- A. Safety Goggles and Chemical-Resistant Gloves
- B. Cricket Helmet
- C. Cotton Cap
- D. Running Shoes

**Correct Answer:** A

**Explanation:**

Acids used in Pickling require workers to wear appropriate chemical-resistant PPE to prevent injuries.

---

# Question 10

### What is the main output of the Pickling process?

- A. Clean stainless steel surface
- B. Molten steel
- C. Finished packaged product
- D. Steel Scrap

**Correct Answer:** A

**Explanation:**

The Pickling process produces a clean, oxide-free stainless steel surface ready for further processing.
