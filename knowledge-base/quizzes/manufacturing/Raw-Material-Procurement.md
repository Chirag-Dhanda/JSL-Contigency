# Question 1

### Which raw material is primarily used to manufacture stainless steel?

- A. Copper
- B. Stainless Steel Scrap
- C. Plastic
- D. Glass

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Stainless steel scrap is the primary raw material used in stainless steel production because it can be recycled efficiently while maintaining material quality.

---

# Question 2

### Which department is mainly responsible for purchasing raw materials?

- A. Human Resources
- B. Procurement
- C. Marketing
- D. Finance

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

The Procurement Department identifies suppliers, negotiates prices, and ensures timely delivery of raw materials.

---

# Question 3

### Why is supplier evaluation important?

- A. To reduce electricity consumption
- B. To ensure consistent quality and timely delivery
- C. To increase warehouse size
- D. To improve product color

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Reliable suppliers help maintain production schedules and ensure that materials meet quality standards.

---

# Question 4

### Which factor is NOT normally checked during raw material inspection?

- A. Material Grade
- B. Chemical Composition
- C. Supplier Documentation
- D. Employee Attendance

**Correct Answer:** D

**Explanation:**

Employee attendance has no relation to the quality inspection of incoming raw materials.

---

# Question 5

### What happens if defective raw material enters production?

- A. Production quality improves
- B. Manufacturing cost decreases
- C. Product quality may be affected
- D. Electricity consumption becomes zero

**Correct Answer:** C

**Explanation:**

Poor-quality raw materials directly affect the quality of finished stainless steel products.

---

# Question 6

### Which document generally accompanies purchased raw material?

- A. Employee ID Card
- B. Material Test Certificate (MTC)
- C. Salary Slip
- D. Attendance Register

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

The Material Test Certificate verifies the chemical and mechanical properties of the supplied material.

---

# Question 7

### Where are raw materials stored after inspection?

- A. Quality Laboratory
- B. Warehouse
- C. Marketing Office
- D. Security Cabin

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Approved raw materials are stored safely in the warehouse before being issued for production.

---

# Question 8

### What is the purpose of inventory management?

- A. Increase paperwork
- B. Maintain the correct quantity of materials
- C. Reduce machine speed
- D. Eliminate production planning

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Proper inventory management ensures uninterrupted production while avoiding excess stock.

---

# Question 9

### Which department verifies the quality of incoming materials?

- A. Quality Assurance
- B. Human Resources
- C. Administration
- D. Sales

**Correct Answer:** A

**Explanation:**

The Quality Assurance team inspects incoming materials before approving them for manufacturing.

---

# Question 10

### What is the first step in the stainless steel manufacturing process?

- A. Grinding
- B. Dispatch
- C. Raw Material Procurement
- D. Pickling

**Correct Answer:** C

**Explanation:**

Every manufacturing cycle begins with procuring quality raw materials from approved suppliers.
