# Question 1

### What is the primary purpose of the Dispatch process?

- A. Melt stainless steel
- B. Deliver finished products safely to customers
- C. Produce molten steel
- D. Inspect raw materials

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Dispatch is the final manufacturing stage where finished products are prepared, documented, and shipped to customers.

---

# Question 2

### Which department works closely with Dispatch to ensure products meet specifications before shipment?

- A. Human Resources
- B. Quality Assurance
- C. Administration
- D. Marketing

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Only products approved by Quality Assurance are dispatched to customers.

---

# Question 3

### Which activity is performed before loading products onto transport vehicles?

- A. Product Packaging
- B. Label Verification
- C. Documentation Review
- D. All of the Above

**Correct Answer:** D

**Explanation:**

Packaging, labeling, and document verification are essential before shipment.

# Question 4

### Which document is commonly prepared during Dispatch?

- A. Delivery Challan
- B. Packing List
- C. Material Test Certificate (MTC)
- D. All of the Above

**Correct Answer:** D

**Explanation:**

These documents provide product details, quality confirmation, and shipping information.

# Question 5

### Why is product traceability important during Dispatch?

- A. To increase warehouse space
- B. To track products from manufacturing to customer delivery
- C. To reduce electricity usage
- D. To improve office attendance

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Traceability allows every dispatched product to be tracked using unique identification numbers.

# Question 6

### Which equipment is commonly used to load heavy stainless steel coils?

- A. Forklift
- B. Overhead Crane
- C. Coil Handling Equipment
- D. All of the Above

**Correct Answer:** D

**Explanation:**

Heavy products require specialized material handling equipment for safe loading.

# Question 7

### What is the main objective of proper packaging?

- A. Improve product appearance only
- B. Protect products during transportation
- C. Increase product weight
- D. Reduce warehouse size

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Packaging prevents damage, corrosion, and contamination during storage and transportation.
