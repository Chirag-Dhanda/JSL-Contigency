# Question 1

### What is the primary purpose of the Grinding process?

- A. To melt stainless steel
- B. To improve surface finish and dimensional accuracy
- C. To package finished products
- D. To cast molten steel

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Grinding removes surface imperfections and improves the finish and dimensional accuracy of stainless steel products.

---

# Question 2

### Which tool is commonly used during the Grinding process?

- A. Acid Tank
- B. Abrasive Grinding Wheel
- C. Electric Arc Furnace
- D. Ladle

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Grinding wheels and abrasive belts remove small amounts of material to produce a smooth surface.

---

# Question 3

### Which manufacturing process usually takes place before Grinding?

- A. Annealing
- B. Raw Material Procurement
- C. Dispatch
- D. Sales

**Correct Answer:** A

**Explanation:**

Annealing restores the material properties, after which Grinding improves the surface finish.

---

# Question 4

### Which defects can be removed by Grinding?

- A. Surface scratches
- B. Weld marks
- C. Minor surface imperfections
- D. All of the Above

**Correct Answer:** D

**Explanation:**

Grinding removes various surface defects to improve the quality and appearance of stainless steel.

---

# Question 5

### What is the main output of the Grinding process?

- A. Molten Steel
- B. Smooth Finished Surface
- C. Scrap Metal
- D. Raw Material

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Grinding produces a smooth and uniform stainless steel surface suitable for inspection or customer use.

---

# Question 6

### Which parameter is important during Grinding?

- A. Surface Finish
- B. Material Removal Rate
- C. Grinding Speed
- D. All of the Above

**Correct Answer:** D

**Explanation:**

Proper control of these parameters ensures consistent quality and prevents damage to the material.

---

# Question 7

### Why is cooling often used during Grinding?

- A. To prevent overheating
- B. To increase rust formation
- C. To reduce steel weight
- D. To improve packaging

**Correct Answer:** A

**Explanation:**

Cooling reduces heat generation, preventing thermal damage and extending tool life.

---

# Question 8

### Which safety equipment is essential while performing Grinding?

- A. Safety Goggles
- B. Face Shield
- C. Gloves and PPE
- D. All of the Above

**Correct Answer:** D

**Explanation:**

Grinding generates sparks, dust, and flying particles, making full PPE essential for worker safety.

---

# Question 9

### Which process generally follows Grinding?

- A. Quality Inspection
- B. Steel Making
- C. Raw Material Procurement
- D. Cold Rolling

**Correct Answer:** A

**Explanation:**

After Grinding, products are inspected to ensure they meet surface finish and dimensional requirements.

---

# Question 10

### Why is Grinding important in stainless steel manufacturing?

- A. It improves appearance
- B. It removes surface defects
- C. It prepares products for final inspection
- D. All of the Above

**Correct Answer:** D

**Explanation:**

Grinding enhances both the appearance and quality of stainless steel products before they are inspected or dispatched.
