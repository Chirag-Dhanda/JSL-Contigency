# Question 1

### What is the primary purpose of Warehouse Management?

- A. Melt stainless steel
- B. Store, manage, and distribute materials efficiently
- C. Perform quality testing
- D. Package finished goods

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Warehouse Management ensures raw materials, work-in-progress, spare parts, and finished goods are stored safely and made available whenever required.

# Question 2

### Which of the following is commonly stored in a manufacturing warehouse?

- A. Raw Materials
- B. Finished Goods
- C. Spare Parts
- D. All of the Above

**Correct Answer:** D

**Explanation:**

Manufacturing warehouses store various categories of inventory including raw materials, WIP, finished goods, and maintenance spares.

# Question 4

### What does FIFO stand for?

- A. First Inventory Final Output
- B. First In, First Out
- C. Fast Inventory First Operation
- D. Final Input First Output

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

FIFO ensures that materials received first are issued first, reducing the risk of aging or obsolete inventory.

# Question 7
