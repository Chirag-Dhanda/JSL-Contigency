# Question 1

### What is the primary purpose of the Electric Arc Furnace?

- A. Cooling steel
- B. Melting stainless steel scrap
- C. Packaging products
- D. Surface polishing

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

The Electric Arc Furnace melts stainless steel scrap using electrical energy to prepare molten metal for further refining.

---

# Question 2

### Which energy source is mainly used in an Electric Arc Furnace?

- A. Diesel
- B. Coal
- C. Electricity
- D. Steam

**Correct Answer:** C

**Explanation:**

The furnace creates an electric arc between graphite electrodes and the metal charge, producing extremely high temperatures.

---

# Question 3

### Which component produces the electric arc?

- A. Cooling Fan

- B. Graphite Electrodes

- C. Water Pump

- D. Conveyor Belt

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Large graphite electrodes generate the electric arc required to melt stainless steel scrap.

---

# Question 4

### Approximately what temperature is achieved inside an Electric Arc Furnace?

- A. 500°C

- B. 900°C

- C. More than 1600°C

- D. 200°C

**Correct Answer:** C

**Explanation:**

The furnace reaches temperatures above 1600°C, allowing complete melting of stainless steel scrap.

# Question 5

### What is the main raw material charged into the Electric Arc Furnace?

- A. Plastic

- B. Iron Ore

- C. Stainless Steel Scrap

- D. Wood

**Correct Answer:** C

**Explanation:**

The EAF primarily uses recycled stainless steel scrap, making the process environmentally sustainable.

---

# Question 6

### Why is slag formed during the melting process?

- A. Decoration

- B. To remove impurities

- C. To increase electricity consumption

- D. To cool the furnace

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Slag absorbs impurities from molten steel, improving its quality before refining.

---

# Question 7

### What happens after the steel is completely melted?

- A. It is immediately dispatched

- B. It is transferred for refining

- C. It is packaged

- D. It is polished

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Molten steel is transferred to refining processes such as AOD for composition adjustment.

---

# Question 8

### Which safety equipment is essential while working near an Electric Arc Furnace?

- A. Sunglasses

- B. PPE including heat-resistant clothing and face shield

- C. Sandals

- D. Cotton gloves only

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Workers require specialized PPE due to extremely high temperatures and molten metal hazards.

---

# Question 9

### Why is electrical power monitoring important in EAF operations?

- A. To improve office lighting

- B. To maintain stable melting conditions

- C. To increase paperwork

- D. To control warehouse inventory

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Stable electrical power ensures efficient melting and prevents furnace interruptions.

---

# Question 10

### Which manufacturing process follows the Electric Arc Furnace?

- A. Dispatch

- B. Annealing

- C. AOD Refining

- D. Grinding

**Correct Answer:** C

**Explanation:**

After melting, molten steel moves to the Argon Oxygen Decarburization process for refining.
