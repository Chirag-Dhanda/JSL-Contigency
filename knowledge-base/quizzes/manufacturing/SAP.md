# Question 1

### What does SAP stand for?

- A. System Application Program
- B. Systems, Applications, and Products in Data Processing
- C. Software and Process Management
- D. Smart Automation Platform

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

SAP stands for Systems, Applications, and Products in Data Processing. It is one of the world's leading Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems.

---

# Question 2

### What is the primary purpose of SAP in a manufacturing organization?

- A. To manufacture steel
- B. To integrate business processes across departments
- C. To control furnace temperature
- D. To transport finished goods

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

SAP connects departments such as Production, Procurement, Quality, Warehouse, Sales, Finance, and Maintenance through a common database.

---

# Question 3

### Which SAP module is responsible for Production Planning?

- A. MM
- B. QM
- C. PP
- D. SD

**Correct Answer:** C

**Explanation:**

SAP PP (Production Planning) manages production orders, scheduling, work centers, and manufacturing planning.

---

# Question 4

### Which SAP module manages purchasing and inventory?

- A. SD
- B. MM
- C. PM
- D. HR

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

SAP MM (Material Management) handles procurement, inventory, goods receipt, and supplier management.

---

# Question 5

### Which SAP module is used for Quality Inspection?

- A. QM
- B. PP
- C. PM
- D. SD

**Correct Answer:** A

**Explanation:**

SAP QM (Quality Management) supports inspection lots, quality notifications, sampling, and test results.

---

# Question 6

### Which SAP module helps manage equipment maintenance?

- A. SD
- B. WM
- C. PM
- D. PP

**Correct Answer:** C

**Explanation:**

SAP PM (Plant Maintenance) manages preventive maintenance, equipment records, and maintenance work orders.

---

# Question 7

### Which SAP module supports customer orders and billing?

- A. PP
- B. SD
- C. MM
- D. QM

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

SAP SD (Sales and Distribution) manages quotations, sales orders, deliveries, shipping, and billing.

---

# Question 8

### What is Master Data in SAP?

- A. Daily production reports
- B. Permanent business information such as Material Master and Customer Master
- C. Employee attendance
- D. Machine alarms

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Master Data contains information that is reused across multiple business processes.

# Question 9

### A production order has been created in SAP PP. Which department is most likely to use this information next?

- A. Production Department
- B. Security Department
- C. Marketing Department
- D. Reception

**Correct Answer:** A

**Explanation:**

Production uses production orders to manufacture products according to planned schedules.

# Question 10

### Why is SAP considered an ERP system?

- A. It controls only one machine.
- B. It integrates multiple business functions into one system.
- C. It replaces PLCs.
- D. It performs quality testing.

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

SAP integrates departments such as Production, Procurement, Sales, Finance, Maintenance, and Quality using one centralized database.
