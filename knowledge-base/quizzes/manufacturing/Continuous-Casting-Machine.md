# Question 1

### What is the primary purpose of the Continuous Casting Machine (CCM)?

- A. Melt scrap
- B. Convert molten steel into semi-finished products
- C. Grind steel surfaces
- D. Package finished products

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

The Continuous Casting Machine converts molten steel into solid slabs, blooms, or billets for further processing.

---

# Question 2

### Which material enters the Continuous Casting Machine?

- A. Stainless Steel Sheets
- B. Molten Steel
- C. Finished Coils
- D. Scrap Metal

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Molten steel from the refining process enters the CCM for solidification.

---

# Question 3

### Which component distributes molten steel into the mould?

- A. Roller Table
- B. Tundish
- C. Grinding Belt
- D. Pickling Tank

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

The tundish acts as an intermediate vessel, ensuring a continuous and controlled flow of molten steel into the mould.

---

# Question 4

### What is the function of the copper mould in a CCM?

- A. Heat the steel
- B. Begin solidifying the molten steel
- C. Polish the surface
- D. Transport the slab

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

The water-cooled copper mould rapidly cools the molten steel, forming a solid outer shell.

---

# Question 5

### Which cooling method is mainly used after the mould?

- A. Air Cooling
- B. Water Spray Cooling
- C. Oil Cooling
- D. Steam Cooling

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Water sprays cool the steel continuously until it solidifies completely.

---

# Question 6

### What products are commonly produced by continuous casting?

- A. Pipes Only
- B. Slabs, Blooms, and Billets
- C. Finished Kitchen Utensils
- D. Steel Powder

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Continuous casting produces semi-finished steel products that are later processed in rolling mills.

---

# Question 7

### Which process generally follows Continuous Casting?

- A. Dispatch
- B. Hot Rolling
- C. Warehouse Storage
- D. Grinding

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

The cast slabs are transferred to the Hot Rolling Mill for further shaping.

---

# Question 8

### Why is continuous casting preferred over traditional ingot casting?

- A. Higher production efficiency
- B. Better product quality
- C. Reduced material waste
- D. All of the above

**Correct Answer:** D

**Explanation:**

Continuous casting offers improved productivity, higher quality, and lower production costs compared to ingot casting.

---

# Question 9

### Which parameter is continuously monitored during casting?

- A. Steel Temperature
- B. Casting Speed
- C. Cooling Rate
- D. All of the above

**Correct Answer:** D

**Explanation:**

Maintaining proper temperature, speed, and cooling ensures defect-free casting.

---

# Question 10

### Why is quality inspection important during continuous casting?

- A. To detect surface and internal defects
- B. To increase electricity usage
- C. To reduce warehouse space
- D. To improve office productivity

**Correct Answer:** A

**Explanation:**

Early detection of defects prevents downstream processing problems and improves final product quality.
