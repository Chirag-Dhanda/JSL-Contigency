# Question 1

### What is Industrial Automation?

- A. Manual operation of machines
- B. Use of technology and control systems to operate industrial processes
- C. Transportation of finished goods
- D. Office management

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Industrial Automation uses control systems such as PLCs, sensors, actuators, and software to operate manufacturing processes with minimal human intervention.

---

# Question 2

### Which of the following is a major benefit of Industrial Automation?

- A. Increased production efficiency
- B. Improved product quality
- C. Reduced human error
- D. All of the Above

**Correct Answer:** D

**Explanation:**

Automation improves efficiency, consistency, safety, and product quality while reducing manual errors.

---

# Question 3

### Which device collects information from the manufacturing process?

- A. Conveyor Belt
- B. Sensor
- C. Forklift
- D. Packaging Machine

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Sensors detect physical conditions such as temperature, pressure, and position, then send this information to the control system.

---

# Question 4

### Which device performs physical actions based on controller commands?

- A. Printer
- B. Actuator
- C. Keyboard
- D. Scanner

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Actuators convert control signals into physical movement, such as opening valves or starting motors.

# Question 5

### Which controller is most commonly used in industrial automation?

- A. Mobile Phone
- B. PLC
- C. Television
- D. Calculator

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs) are the most widely used industrial controllers for automation.

# Question 6
