# Question 1

### What is the primary purpose of the Hot Rolling process?

- A. To package steel products
- B. To reduce the thickness and shape heated steel
- C. To polish the surface
- D. To inspect product quality

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Hot Rolling reduces the thickness of steel and forms it into the required shape while the steel remains at a high temperature.

---

# Question 2

### At what condition is steel processed during Hot Rolling?

- A. Room Temperature
- B. Frozen Condition
- C. High Temperature above the recrystallization temperature
- D. Under Water

**Correct Answer:** C

**Explanation:**

Steel is rolled while it is hot, making it easier to deform without cracking.

---

# Question 3

### Which equipment is mainly responsible for reducing the thickness of steel?

- A. Grinding Machine
- B. Rolling Mill
- C. Pickling Tank
- D. Warehouse Crane

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Rolling mills contain heavy rollers that compress the heated steel to the desired thickness.

---

# Question 4

### Which product enters the Hot Rolling Mill?

- A. Molten Steel
- B. Steel Slab
- C. Finished Coil
- D. Scrap Metal

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Steel slabs produced by the Continuous Casting Machine are reheated and then sent to the Hot Rolling Mill.

---

# Question 5

### Which process occurs before Hot Rolling?

- A. Grinding
- B. Dispatch
- C. Continuous Casting
- D. Pickling

**Correct Answer:** C

**Explanation:**

Continuous Casting produces the slabs that are later rolled during Hot Rolling.

---

# Question 6

### What is the main output of the Hot Rolling process?

- A. Steel Powder
- B. Hot Rolled Coil
- C. Stainless Steel Scrap
- D. Iron Ore

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

The finished product of this process is generally a Hot Rolled Coil (HRC).

---

# Question 7

### Why are reheating furnaces used before rolling?

- A. To cool the steel
- B. To increase steel temperature for easier rolling
- C. To inspect steel
- D. To package products

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

The slabs are reheated to ensure they are soft enough for rolling.

---

# Question 8

### Which parameter is closely monitored during Hot Rolling?

- A. Rolling Speed
- B. Temperature
- C. Thickness
- D. All of the above

**Correct Answer:** D

**Explanation:**

Proper control of speed, temperature, and thickness ensures high-quality rolled products.

---

# Question 9

### Which process usually follows Hot Rolling?

- A. Pickling
- B. Dispatch
- C. Warehouse Storage
- D. Sales

**Correct Answer:** A

**Explanation:**

Hot rolled steel passes through the Pickling process to remove oxide scale from the surface.

---

# Question 10

### Why is quality inspection important after Hot Rolling?

- A. To verify dimensions and surface quality
- B. To reduce electricity consumption
- C. To increase warehouse space
- D. To prepare employee reports

**Correct Answer:** A

**Explanation:**

Inspection ensures that the rolled steel meets dimensional and quality specifications before moving to the next process.
