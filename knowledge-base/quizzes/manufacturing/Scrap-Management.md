# Question 1

### What is the primary purpose of Scrap Management?

- A. Increase production waste
- B. Efficiently collect, classify, store, and recycle scrap
- C. Package finished products
- D. Transport employees

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Scrap Management ensures valuable stainless steel scrap is collected, sorted, and reused efficiently during manufacturing.

---

# Question 2

### Why is stainless steel scrap considered valuable?

- A. It has no use after production
- B. It can be recycled and reused as raw material
- C. It is only used for decoration
- D. It is disposed of immediately

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Scrap is a major raw material in stainless steel manufacturing and helps reduce production costs.

---

# Question 3

### Which of the following is an example of internal scrap?

- A. Purchased scrap from suppliers
- B. Process offcuts generated during manufacturing
- C. Imported raw material
- D. Finished steel coils

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Internal scrap is generated during manufacturing processes such as trimming and cutting.

---

# Question 4

### Why is scrap segregation important?

- A. To mix all materials together
- B. To prevent contamination and maintain quality
- C. To increase transportation time
- D. To reduce warehouse space

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Segregating scrap by grade and composition prevents contamination and improves steel quality.

---

# Question 5

### Which process uses recycled scrap as one of its primary raw materials?

- A. Grinding
- B. Electric Arc Furnace (EAF)
- C. Dispatch
- D. Warehouse

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

The Electric Arc Furnace melts stainless steel scrap to produce molten steel.

---

# Question 6

### Before charging scrap into the furnace, it is inspected for:

- A. Contamination
- B. Material Grade
- C. Moisture
- D. All of the Above

**Correct Answer:** D

**Explanation:**

Inspection ensures only suitable scrap is used for steel production.

---

# Question 7

### Which practice improves inventory tracking of scrap?

- A. Barcode or RFID Tracking
- B. Random Storage
- C. Manual Guesswork
- D. Mixing All Grades

**Correct Answer:** A

**Explanation:**

Barcode and RFID systems improve traceability and inventory accuracy.

# Question 8

### What is one environmental benefit of scrap recycling?

- A. Increased mining
- B. Higher pollution
- C. Reduced consumption of natural resources
- D. Increased industrial waste

**Correct Answer:** C

**Explanation:**

Recycling scrap reduces the need for mining and conserves natural resources.

# Question 9

### Which safety practice should be followed while handling scrap?

- A. Ignore PPE
- B. Wear appropriate Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)
- C. Walk under suspended loads
- D. Stack materials without planning

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Proper PPE and safe handling procedures reduce the risk of workplace injuries.

# Question 10

### What is the main objective of effective Scrap Management?

- A. Increase waste generation
- B. Improve recycling efficiency and reduce manufacturing costs
- C. Eliminate quality inspections
- D. Delay production

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Effective Scrap Management supports sustainable manufacturing, improves quality, and lowers production costs.
