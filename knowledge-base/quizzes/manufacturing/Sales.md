# Question 1

### What is the primary role of the Sales department in a manufacturing organization?

- A. Melt stainless steel
- B. Manage customer orders and coordinate product delivery
- C. Repair machinery
- D. Inspect raw materials

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

The Sales department manages customer inquiries, processes orders, coordinates with production, and ensures products are delivered on time.

---

# Question 2

### Which activity usually begins the sales process?

- A. Dispatch
- B. Customer Inquiry
- C. Packaging
- D. Warehouse Storage

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

The sales process starts when a customer requests information about products, pricing, or delivery schedules.

---

# Question 3

### Which document is generally prepared before a Sales Order is created?

- A. Salary Slip
- B. Quotation
- C. Attendance Register
- D. Purchase Order

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

A quotation provides pricing, specifications, and delivery terms. Once accepted, it is converted into a Sales Order.

---

# Question 4

### Which SAP module primarily supports Sales activities?

- A. PP
- B. MM
- C. SD
- D. PM

**Correct Answer:** C

**Explanation:**

SAP SD (Sales and Distribution) manages quotations, sales orders, deliveries, shipping, and billing.

---

# Question 5

### Which department works closely with Sales to manufacture customer orders?

- A. Human Resources
- B. Production Planning
- C. Security
- D. Administration

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Production Planning schedules manufacturing activities based on confirmed customer orders.

---

# Question 6

### Which activity takes place before products are dispatched to customers?

- A. Quality Inspection
- B. Packaging
- C. Shipping Documentation
- D. All of the Above

**Correct Answer:** D

**Explanation:**

Products must pass quality inspection, be packaged correctly, and have complete documentation before dispatch.

---

# Question 7

### Why is customer relationship management important?

- A. To increase product defects
- B. To build long-term customer satisfaction and loyalty
- C. To reduce production capacity
- D. To eliminate quality checks

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Maintaining good customer relationships encourages repeat business and strengthens the company's reputation.

---

# Question 8

### Which document is commonly generated after successful product delivery?

- A. Invoice
- B. Employee ID Card
- C. Work Permit
- D. Machine Manual

**Correct Answer:** A

**Explanation:**

An invoice is generated for billing after products are dispatched or delivered according to company procedures.

---

# Question 9

### What is one common challenge faced by the Sales department?

- A. Customer complaints
- B. Delivery delays
- C. Product availability
- D. All of the Above

**Correct Answer:** D

**Explanation:**

Sales teams regularly manage customer expectations, delivery schedules, and product availability.

---

# Question 10

### What is the primary objective of Sales and Customer Order Management?

- A. Increase warehouse inventory
- B. Ensure customer requirements are fulfilled accurately and on time
- C. Control furnace temperature
- D. Operate rolling mills

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**

Sales aims to deliver the right product, in the right quantity, at the right time while maintaining customer satisfaction.
